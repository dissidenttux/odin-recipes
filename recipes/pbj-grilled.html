Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

"Peanut butter and jelly sandwich" by Matias-Garabedian is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0 .

Description

A toasty take on a globalized classic. This peanut butter and jelly will warm up your mood from the inside out. The slightest touch of crispy, the highest returns.

Ingredients

  • Butter (2 teaspoons)
  • White bread (2 slices)
  • Jelly (Any fruit-based flavor)
  • Peanut Butter (1 teaspoon)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients, and preheat a griddle (or skillet) to around 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread peanut butter on the other side, of one of the slices of bread. Spread jelly on the other side, of the other slice of bread.
  3. Place the slices of bread together in an orientation where the sides covered with plain butter is exposed on the outside.
  4. Cook on the griddle on each side until golden brown and heated through (approx. 4 minutes per side).
